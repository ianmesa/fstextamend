About this map

This interactive map visualizes the City of Mesa’s proposed Fleet-Based Services & Service Stations zoning text amendment (amending Mesa City Code Title 11, Chapters 6, 7, 8, 31 and 86). Pick a use classification, then flip between Today’s Code, the Proposed amendment, and What Changes to see exactly which zoning districts are affected.

How to read it

Green = permitted by right · Hatched amber = requires a Council Use Permit (CUP) · Gray = not permitted. In the What Changes view, blue marks districts where the use is already allowed today with no change. Click any area for its district-by-district status; dashed black lines are City Council districts.

Sources

  • Current code: Mesa Zoning Ordinance (Title 11).
  • Zoning & council district boundaries: City of Mesa GIS, loaded live at page open.

Unofficial visualization prepared for public engagement and board discussion. It is not a legal document; where this map and the adopted ordinance text differ, the ordinance governs. Overlay districts and site-specific conditions are not shown.
